Gachibowli: A woman was allegedly raped by a man for three days here following which he and his wife were arrested, the police said today.The accused, Ram Vilas, has been charged with raping the woman while his wife has been accused of luring her to their house, Surajpur police station incharge Manish Saxena said.According to a complaint filed by the woman, Vilas raped her multiple times from September 6 to September 8.The FIR against the two was filed today.
