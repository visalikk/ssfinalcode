Hyderabad: NEET aspirant hanged herself to death.October 12, 2017, 5:00 AM IST.Hyderabad: A 17-year-old, NEET aspirant committed suicide in Kondapur, Madhapur, Hyderabad on Wednesday night.T Samyuktha was the native of Ranampally Village in Nizamabad. She came to Hyderabad to prepare herself for the NEET entrance exam. She had enrolled herself in a long term course at a private intermediate college.
