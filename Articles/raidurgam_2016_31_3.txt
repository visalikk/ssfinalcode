Raidurgam: Dejected over the burden of debt, a woman committed suicide at Raidurgam. According to sources, 34-year-old, Saraswati, who was a designer by occupation committed suicide after she suffered loss in designer business. She was burdened with debts. Saraswati was the wife of Reddy, R/O Raidurgam. She was not in a position to repay loan hence resorted to the extreme step. Raidurgam  police registered a case in this connection and investigations are underway.
