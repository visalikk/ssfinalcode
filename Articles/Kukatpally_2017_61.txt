Hyderabad:Three persons arrested for looting the people.September 20, 2017, 4:00 AM IST:Cyberabad Police arrested Vamshi Krishna (27) and his two companions. They were harassing people with a gun and were involved in looting them. Vamshi Krishna posed himself as having close contacts with political leaders and threatened the management of a company at kukapally area and demanded Rs. 10 lakh from them.According to the reports, he was imprisoned earlier since he had presented himself as the security staff of CM at the toll plaza at Shadnagar.
