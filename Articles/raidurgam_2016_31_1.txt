Raidurgam: A drunkard man burned his wife alive suspecting infidelity at Raidurgam. The incident occurred under the jurisdiction of Raidurgam police station. According to police Anjali 28 died of burns as her husband set her on fire. Her husband Kankanaya suspected infidelity and used to fight with his wife daily after coming home drunk. On August 27 he went to his relatives, while his wife was alone in his house. He returned home in inebriated condition and quarreled with his wife. Later he poured kerosene over her and set her ablaze. She was rushed to the hospital where she succumbed to burns yesterday.
