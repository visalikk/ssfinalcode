Gachibowli: A 25-year-old man has committed suicide by jumping in front of a train, after allegedly murdering his newly-wed wife in Uttar Pradeshs Bijnor district, police said today.The incident happened in the afternoon in Gachibowli village, when Ankit slit the throat of his 22-year-old wife, Nidhi, with a sharp weapon and fled the spot. His mutilated body was later found nearby on the railway tracks, which signified he was probably run over by a train, an official said.at 12.00 mid night
