Gachibouli:  A 38-year-old woman was thrashed, doused with petrol and set on fire near her home in Hyderabad on Monday morning, allegedly by eight people including her neighbour who is 17.According to the police, the woman - a domestic help - had a heated argument with her neighbour and his family on Sunday night over some construction material, including iron rods, placed in front of her house in a slum area of Gachibouli.
On Monday morning, the neighbor and seven others - his associates according to the police - allegedly attacked the woman, poured petrol on her and set her on fire.
