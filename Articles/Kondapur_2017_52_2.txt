Hyderabad:Trainee woman constable hangs self at RBVRR Telangana Police Academy.October 9, 2017, 7:55 AM IST.A 23-year-old trainee woman constable committed suicide by hanging herself at the RBVRR Telangana Police Academy on Saturday night.According to the Station House Officer (SHO) of the Narsinghi Police Station, the trainee constable, Naveena, took the drastic step following the suicide of her close friend Madhavi, who worked as a constable at the Vemulapalli Police Station of the Nalgonda district.Naveena is the resident of Kondapur
