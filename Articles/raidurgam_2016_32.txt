Raidurgam: Raidurgam police recovered the body of a man from local nala. The man has been identified as Shafeeq. Police told that Shafeeq aged around 30 years was said to be a driver by occupation. He resided at Sanath Nagar. On learning about the incident police retrieved the body. However reasons behind Shafeeqs death could not be ascertained. Police is investigating the case.
