Hyderabad:Businessmen prefer train journey for Gold smuggling.September 27, 2017, 7:05 AM IST.With the apprehension of catching, smugglers prefer train journey to road journey or air travel. SP of Railway Police, Mr. S. Rajindra Prasad told that after getting information,security arrangements were made strict. He further told that it is not possible to check each passenger at railway stations.
