Hyderabad blasts: Guilty will not go unpunished, says PM
February 22, 2013 07:04 IST
New Delhi:  The Prime Minister has described the twin bomb blasts in Hyderabad on Thursday evening as a dastardly attack. Tweets from his office vowed, The guilty will not go unpunished. 11 people have been killed and over 70 injured after bombs placed on bicycles exploded near two cinemas in the busy Dilsukhnagar area of Hyderabad.
