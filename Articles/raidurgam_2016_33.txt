Raidurgam: Six people including patients were injured at state-run Osmania hospital in Hyderabad, after a car belonging to a doctor ran into a group of patients and others near the hospitals parking lot. 2 patients were injured in the accident, including a 95-year-old Md Omer, whose leg has been crushed and Qamer Begum, 55, who was suffering from heart ailment and had come to the hospital for treatment resident of Raidurgam.
