Hyderabad: 14-year-old girl commits suicide after school humiliates her for non-payment of fees
February 2, 2018 12:59 IST
A 14-year-old girl named Sai Deepti committed suicide by hanging herself with the ceiling fan at her house in Malkajgiri after her school humiliated her for not paying fees on time. Deepti was studying in Class 9 at Jyothi High School, Malkajgiri.
