Hyderabad:Engineering student commits suicide.October 5, 2017, 14:00 IST.In Kondapur,an engineering student committed suicide.According to the sources,a 19-year-old boy, Ramesh who was a student of 1st year of engineering course committed suicide. It is suspected that he might have committed suicide two or three days back in his hostel room. 
When police got information, it reached the spot and took out the dead body. This suicide created a sensation among the locals
