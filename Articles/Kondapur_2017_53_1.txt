Hyderabad:Man dies in road accident.October 12, 2017, 10:14 AM IST.Hyderabad:In a terrific road accident occurred in kondapur area, a man died.According to the reports of the police, in kondapur a fruit vendor, Yadaiah (60), a resident of Abdullahpur died on the spot in an accident.He was crossing the road at Kattalur crossroads in the morning on Wednesday.A fast-moving lorry crushed him.
