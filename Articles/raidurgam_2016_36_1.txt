Raidurgam: Three persons belonging to Bangladesh who had overstayed in Hyderabad were taken into police custody earlier were released on Sunday and a case has been registered.According to the details, Mohammed Abdul Malik (40) and his two sons were reported to have overstayed in Hyderabad and they were also suspected to be the agents of ISIS as was indicated by NIA and UP Police but Commissioner of Police, Raidurgam, Mr. Sandeep Shindalia released them after investigations and registered a case against them under section 41 of CrPC. After verification of their citizenship, further action would be taken.
