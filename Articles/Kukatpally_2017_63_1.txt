Hyderabad:Hyderabad Marriage Racket: Main accused Qazi, Arab sheikhs arrested.September 26, 2017, 11:23 AM IST.Hyderabad: Continuing their crackdown on the child bride marriage racket, the Hyderabad Police nabbed a Qazi and three more sheikhs from Arab in connection with the case.As per a report, the Qazi is believed to be the main accused in the child bride racket case.On September 20, the police busted a marriage racket in the city and arrested eight sheikhs and four Muslim clerics for being part of the plot. The arrested sheikhs hail from Saudi Arabia, Oman and Qatar.The clerics were held on the charges of cheating innocent girls in the name of marriage.
