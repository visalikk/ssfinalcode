Hyderabad:Trio died, as triple riders bike rammed into divider.September 25, 2017, 12:00 IST :In a horrific incident occurred at kukatpally, three persons riding a motorcycle died in an accident.According to inspector Mr. P Jagdeshwar, Upendar (25) resident of vivekananda Nagar along with his two friends, Mohammed Moin (42) resident of Bandlaguda and Husain Shah alias Zakir (40) was heading towards Pallecheruvu from Katedhan on a bike. The vehicle lost balance at kukatpally before Muthoot Finance office and rammed into a divider. Riders suffered head injuries. They died while they were being shifted to the hospital.
