Raidurgam: A minor girl was allegedly raped by a 60-year-old man in Raidurgam area here, police said today.The accused, B Saidaiah, who stays near the victims house, was arrested today for the crime that took place today, they said. The accused is a labourer. He went to the girls house (when her parents were not present) yesterday afternoon and lured her by offering chocolates. Thereafter, he took the girl to his house and allegedly raped her, they said. The girl informed about the matter to her parents, after which they caught hold of the accused and handed over him to police today. They also lodged a complaint, a police official attached to Kushaiguda Police Station said.
